# Privacy Policy

**Bubble Sheet Auto-Mark** is a fully offline application. It does not collect, transmit, or
store any personal information outside of your own device.

## What the app accesses

| Resource | Why |
|---|---|
| **Camera** | To capture bubble-sheet images for grading. Images are processed in memory and are never saved unless you explicitly export them. |
| **Local storage** | To read image files you import and to write CSV grade exports that you initiate. |

## What the app does NOT do

- Does not connect to the internet.
- Does not send images, grade data, or any other information to any server or cloud service.
- Does not include analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry of any kind.
- Does not create user accounts or require any login.

## Your data stays with you

Any CSV files exported by the app are created on your device and remain entirely under your
control. The app has no access to those files after you save them.

Student images captured during grading are processed in memory only. No image is written to
disk by the app unless your operating system or another application does so independently.

## Regulatory compatibility

Because no personal information leaves the device, Bubble Sheet Auto-Mark is designed to be
compatible with student-data privacy obligations under:

- **FERPA** (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act, USA)
- **FIPPA** (Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act, Canada)

Institutions remain responsible for ensuring that their own use of exported grade data complies
with applicable regulations.

## Questions or concerns

If you have questions about privacy, please open an issue in the
[GitHub repository](https://github.com/wdconinc/bubble-sheet-auto-mark/issues).
